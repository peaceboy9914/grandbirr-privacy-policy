# Privacy Policy – GrandBirr Business

Effective date: 16 February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **GrandBirr Business – Referral and Investor Management App** (“GrandBirr Business”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

If you have any questions, please contact us at **mahdinurazaman@gmail.com**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use GrandBirr Business:

### 1.1 Personal Information

- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **User ID** (for example, Firebase user ID and internal account ID)
- **Phone number** (optional, including for verification/OTP)
- **Profile photo / avatar** (optional)
- **Addresses** (for example, street, city, state, postal code, country)

### 1.2 Photos and Uploads

- **Deposit receipt images** you upload when submitting Islamic finance deposits.
- **Profile pictures / avatars** you choose to upload.

### 1.3 App Activity and Usage

- Actions you perform in the App, such as:
  - Creating and managing referrals
  - Submitting deposits or investment requests
  - Requesting withdrawals
  - Using MLM/referral features and dashboards
- Basic usage information (for example, screens visited, features used) to help us operate and improve the App.

### 1.4 Device and Other Identifiers

- **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token** or similar identifiers used to send push notifications to your device.

We do **not** intentionally collect sensitive categories such as race or ethnicity, political opinions, religious beliefs, sexual orientation, or biometric identifiers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### 2.1 App Functionality

- To create and manage your **user account**.
- To enable **referral**, **MLM**, and **investment** features.
- To process and review **Islamic finance deposits** and **withdrawal requests**.
- To show your profile, referral codes, and network statistics in the App.

### 2.2 Account Management

- To authenticate and log you in (email/password, Google Sign‑In, phone verification).
- To maintain your account settings and profile information.
- To manage roles such as customer, employee, admin, and super admin.

### 2.3 Communications

- To send **transactional notifications**, such as:
  - Deposit submission and status updates
  - Withdrawal status updates
  - Other important information about your account or security
- To respond to your requests, questions, or feedback.

### 2.4 Analytics and Improvement

- To understand how users interact with the App and its features.
- To monitor performance, quality, and reliability.
- To diagnose and fix technical issues.

### 2.5 Fraud Prevention, Security, and Compliance

- To verify deposits and withdrawals (for example, using receipt images).
- To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized activity.
- To comply with legal, regulatory, or accounting obligations where applicable.

We **do not** use your data to show personalized third‑party advertisements inside the App.

## 3. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

Depending on your location, our legal bases for processing your personal data may include:

- **Performance of a contract** (providing the App and its features to you).
- **Legitimate interests** (for example, securing our services, improving the App, preventing fraud).
- **Consent** (for example, optional features like notifications or profile photos, where required by law).

We do **not** sell your personal information.

We may share your information with:

### 4.1 Service Providers

- **Firebase (Google)** for:
  - Authentication
  - Cloud Firestore database
  - Cloud Storage for images
  - Cloud Messaging for push notifications
- Other technical providers that help us host and operate the App and backend.

These providers process data **on our behalf** to deliver the App’s functionality and are required to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws and our agreements with them.

### 4.2 Business and Legal Requirements

We may disclose information where we believe it is necessary to:

- Comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.
- Respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- Protect the rights, property, or safety of GrandBirr Business, our users, or others.
- Detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues.

We do **not** share your data with advertising networks or other third parties for their own marketing purposes.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide the App and related services to you.
- Fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.
- Comply with legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.
- Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.

When your data is no longer needed for these purposes, we will delete it or anonymize it so that it can no longer be linked to you.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have some or all of the following rights:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal information.
- **Correction**: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
- **Deletion**: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal limitations.
- **Restriction**: Request that we limit the processing of your information in certain cases.
- **Objection**: Object to certain types of processing (for example, where we rely on legitimate interests).
- **Data portability**: Request a copy of your data in a commonly used format where applicable.

You can exercise some of these rights directly in the App (for example, by editing your profile or deleting your account), or by contacting us at **mahdinurazaman@gmail.com**.
